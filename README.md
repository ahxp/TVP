Privacy Policy for TVP Player
Last updated: January 31, 2026
Introduction
TVP Player is an IPTV client application that allows users to watch live TV streams. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information.
Information We Collect
TVP Player does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to external servers. All data remains on your device.
Data Stored Locally
The following information may be stored locally on your device only:

Playlist URLs or M3U files you add
Channel favorites and preferences
App settings

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.
Third-Party Content
TVP Player is a player only. We do not provide, host, or control any streaming content. Users are responsible for the playlists and streams they add to the app.
Analytics and Tracking
We do not use any analytics, tracking, or advertising services.
Children's Privacy
TVP Player does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.
